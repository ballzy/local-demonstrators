Local healthcare providers are helping patients to see their personal health records online. Patients can use their records to:

  • see information that healthcare staff want to share with them
  • find out about appointments and treatment
  • have more control over their health problems
  • bring together information from different NHS organisations they have contact with

The number of services on this page will increase as more NHS organisations develop digital services for patients in their areas.

Birmingham

If you are an inpatient or outpatient receiving care at the Queen Elizabeth Hospital Birmingham, you can access myhealth@QEHB to:

  • view your secure healthcare information online
  • share information with the health professional of your choice
  • get more control over your care within the NHS
  • improve your understanding of your condition and its management

Southampton

If you are an inpatient or outpatient receiving care at University Hospital Southampton (UHS), you can access My medical record to:

  • view your UHS hospital medical record
  • take part in online consultations for specific conditions
  • share information with doctors and other healthcare professionals
  • reduce the need for hospital visits
